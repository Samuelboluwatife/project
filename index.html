About us

At C.W.T., we believe true fitness goes beyond aesthetics—it's about longevity, skill, and functional strength rooted in connective tissue health and muscle growth. Whether you're recovering from joint issues or simply seeking to move through life with power and ease, our programs are designed to help you thrive

We specialize in:

Our foundation is Christian, Tried, and True, guided by the belief that caring for the body is part of spiritual stewardship (see 3 John 2). While our values are faith-based, our doors are open to anyone seeking freedom of movement and a healthier life.

Meet Our Founder

Elugbaju Olatide

Olatide is an athlete with 5+ years experience. He specializes in corrective rehabilitation, functional strength( through kettlebells and calisthenics), and body recomposition. He aims to get every client to their goals and maximize quality of life. When he is not coaching, he works as a registered nurse, continuing to fulfill his passion: collaborating with people for their health and wellbeing.
